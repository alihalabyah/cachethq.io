What is Cachet?

Cachet is an open source status page system, written in PHP with the Laravel 4.2 framework. Having been developed to support hobby projects, small business to a large enterprise, Cachet is the perfect solution to your status updates.

What does it do?

  • Keeps your users, customers or developers up to date with service level information
  • Supports customization at a theme level and supplying a complete stylesheet
  • Comes with a beautiful and responsive dashboard for adding incidents from anywhere
  • Bundled with a RESTful API for making your own integrations

What is it not?

Cachet is not a system health checker, it doesn't monitor your services and update when something has gone wrong. For something like that, you could check out Uptime Robot and Pingdom.
